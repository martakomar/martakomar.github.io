Pacific Northwest Ocean

My experience in Washington

My experience with Washington is just home. I love the cool fresh ocean air. For me, it's the most ideal getaway. We pack blankets, chairs, wood, hotdogs, and smores and always have the best time. I enjoy that the grey sky reflects the water. I love the cliffs and venturing out along the shore. It's my favorite place in our Pacific Northwest.
A few things that come to my head when I think of our Washington beach:
  • Seagulls
  • Salty air
  • CALM
  • Tents
  • Good company
  • Bonfire
  • Starfish
